3. Core Definitions & Scope (Part II, Chapter 2)

* Applicability: International flights performed by aeroplanes with a Maximum Take-off Mass (MTOW) exceeding $5,700\text{ kg}$.
* Exemption: Aeroplane operators with annual $CO_{2}$ emissions from international flights $\le 10,000$ tonnes.
* State of Registry: The State on whose register the aeroplane is entered.
* Aeroplane Operator: A person, organization or enterprise engaged in or offering to engage in an aeroplane operation.
